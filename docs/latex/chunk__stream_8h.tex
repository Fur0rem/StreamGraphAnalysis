\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A Stream that represents a chunk of a Stream\+Graph. It contains a subset of the nodes and links of the Stream\+Graph. It also contains a subset of the time present in the Stream\+Graph. ~\newline
 It is useful to work on a subset of the Stream\+Graph. For example, to work on a specific time interval or on a specific set of nodes. ~\newline
 It is optimised to work on a big chunk of the Stream\+Graph. If you want to work on a small chunk of the Stream\+Graph, maybe Chunk\+Stream\+Small will be more efficient.
