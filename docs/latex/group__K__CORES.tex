\doxysubsubsection{Detailed Description}
Compute the k-\/cores of a stream. 

The k-\/core of a stream graph is the largest sub-\/stream of nodes where each node is connected to at least k other nodes during a maximal time interval. \begin{DoxySeeAlso}{See also}
Section 13 of the paper for more information.
\end{DoxySeeAlso}
