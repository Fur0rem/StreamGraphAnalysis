double SGA_Stream_robustness_by_duration ( SGA_Stream * stream)

Computes the robustness of a stream by the duration of the walks in it. The robustness (by duration) of a stream is the average duration of all optimal walks between all nodes at all times. It represents how much time is needed on average to go from a node to another node at a given time in the stream.
